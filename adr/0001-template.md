# ADR-NNNN: Short, present-tense decision title

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by [ADR-NNNN](nnnn-title.md) | Deprecated
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** Names or roles
- **Tags:** area, topic

## Context

The forces at play: the problem, constraints, and assumptions. State the facts
and the tension between options. Keep it neutral — this section explains *why a
decision is needed*, not which one we made.

## Decision

The decision, in the active voice: "We will …". Be specific and unambiguous. One
decision per ADR.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result. Include positive,
negative, and neutral consequences, plus any follow-up work or new risks this
decision introduces.

## Alternatives considered

Each viable option that was rejected, with a one-line reason it lost. This is what
makes an ADR trustworthy months later.

## References

Links to related ADRs, official documentation, issues, or discussions.
